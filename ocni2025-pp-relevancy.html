Fit tier B — possible buyer or strong influence (specifier, channel, project pull). Worth meeting if time allows.
Low
Fit tier C — weak or no clear tubular demand for PP. Keep for context; do not prioritize.
Peer sellers
Peer pipe / metals sellers (tier D). Treat as possible distributors we can supply — and map who their customers are (those end buyers are also PP targets).
